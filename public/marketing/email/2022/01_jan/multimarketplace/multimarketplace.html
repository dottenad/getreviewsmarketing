Introducing

Multi-Marketplace Reviews

Collect reviews on marketplaces that matter to your business

Utilize GetReviews.ai's best-in-class review funnel software to collect reviews on any and all marketplaces that matter to your business

To enable a new marketplace, visit your Marketplace Library and enable the marketplace that you would like to begin collecting reviews on. Once a marketplace is enabled, a new product ID field will show up for that marketplace on your products. If a product has a marketplace ID, and if that product is enabled on a survey, customers will be allowed to review and claim their giveaway pending a valid order number for that marketplace.

Note: New marketplaces are currently added by GetReviews administrators, so if there is a marketplace you would like to begin collecting reviews on that isn't currently available in the Marketplace Library, please reach out to us and we will get it added.

Introducing

Order Number Validator

Enable automatic order number validation on all marketplaces

Automate the validation of order numbers with the new order number validator.

To enable order number validation, visit the Orders Center and upload a CSV containing order numbers for the marketplace that you would like to validate customer order numbers against. If an order number exists in the selected marketplace, the customers order number will validate and they will be allowed to review their product and claim their giveaway. If no order number is detected, the customer will be shown an error.

Note: You can only upload order numbers for marketplaces that are enabled on your account. If you do not see the marketplace that you would like to upload order numbers for, please visit the Marketplace Library and enable the marketplace you would like to upload order numbers for.
